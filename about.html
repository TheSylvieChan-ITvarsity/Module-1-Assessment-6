About Us



We are a small sole proprietorship who is passionate about making high quality and affordable plushies for our local South-Africans.

Finding good quality plushies nowadays can be a daunting task, and more often than not we receive sub-par products that seem to fall apart at the seams.

We took it upon ourselves to do better and to provide our local community with the quality they deserve.

Established quite literally yesterday, we are driven to give our customers the utmost assurance that we are reputable and eager to get our collective feet through the door of e-commerce. Our infrastructure may be small but we are committed to growing our business and establishing a loyal customer base.
